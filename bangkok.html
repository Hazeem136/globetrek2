About Bangkok

Bangkok, Thailand's vibrant capital, is a captivating blend of ancient traditions and modern dynamism, famous for its ornate temples, bustling street life, rich cuisine, and towering skyscrapers, all centered around the Chao Phraya River

Culture

Buddhism

The dominant religion shapes architecture, daily life, and values, seen in countless temples, Buddha statues, and rituals like making merit at Wats.
