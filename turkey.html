About Turkey

A country bridging Europe and Asia, known for its rich history, diverse culture, historic sites, and beautiful landscapes, with Istanbul as its cultural hub.

Culture

Cuisine

Famous for dishes like kebabs, baklava, and Turkish tea, reflecting regional flavors and traditions.

Traditional Craft

Turkey is known for carpets, ceramics, and calligraphy, showcasing its rich artistic heritage.



Symbols of Turkey

Hagia Sophia

A historic landmark in Istanbul, Turkey, originally built as a church, later converted into a mosque, and now a museum. It is famous for its massive dome, stunning mosaics, and Byzantine architecture, symbolizing Turkey‚Äôs rich cultural and religious history.

Cappadocia

A unique region in central Turkey known for its fairy-tale landscapes, rock formations, and cave dwellings. It is famous for hot air balloon rides at sunrise, offering panoramic views of valleys, ancient cave churches, and historical underground cities.



Places to Visit

Pamukkale

A natural wonder in southwestern Turkey, famous for its white travertine terraces formed by mineral-rich hot springs. Visitors can bathe in the warm pools and explore the nearby ancient city of Hierapolis, making it a unique combination of nature and history.

Ephesus

An ancient city in Turkey, known for its well-preserved Roman ruins, including the Library of Celsus and the Grand Theatre. It was a major center of trade and culture in antiquity and is now a popular historical and tourist site.
