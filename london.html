About London

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom. It is famous for its history, landmarks like Big Ben and Buckingham Palace, and modern attractions like the London Eye. The city is lively, with many museums, theaters, and a mix of cultures from around the world.

Symbols of London

Big Ben

Big Ben is the famous clock tower in London, known for its huge bell and iconic architecture. It is a symbol of the city and part of the Palace of Westminster.

Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace in London is the official home of the British monarch. It is famous for its royal ceremonies and the Changing of the Guard.
